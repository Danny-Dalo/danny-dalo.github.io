The Problem
Most DTC brands have
a Klaviyo account.
Not a retention strategy.
  • Broadcasting the same email to every customer regardless of how recently or how often they buy
  • No visibility into which customers are about to churn — until they already have
  • Klaviyo flows built once and never revisited against real purchase behavior
  • Pouring budget into acquisition while the existing customer base leaks quietly
  • Tracking LTV and AOV as vanity metrics rather than as signals for action
  • No way to tell which 20% of customers are driving 60% of the revenue

Most retention problems aren't caused by bad products or weak offers. They're caused by not knowing which customers to prioritize, what to say to them, and when to say it.
